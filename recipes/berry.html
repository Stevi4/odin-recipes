Berry Compote

Description

Berry compote is a sweet and fruity treat on its own, or a good pairing with other foods like ice cream or pancakes. This recipe is a good way to make use of mixed berries.

Ingredients

  • 6 c. frozen mixed berries
  • 1/2 c. white sugar
  • 1/4 c. orange juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp. finely grated orange zest
  • 2 tbs. cornstarch
  • 2 tbs. water

Steps

  1. Stir frozen berries, sugar, orange juice, and orange zest in a slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on high heat until bubbling (~1 1/2 hours).
  3. Stir cornstarch and water together in a cup until fully dissolved, then stir into berry mixture.
  4. Cover and cook again until thickened (~5-10 minutes).
  5. Serve warm or at room temparature.
